Must-have features of artiManage that every business needs

  • Artificial intelligence

    AI assistant, ArtI, can help your sales team with predictive sales, and search for things like a customer or a conversation with an employee with full context.

  • Business intelligence

    Generate reports easily, detect anomalies in sales or support, analyze trends, and generate insights from spreadsheets through ArtI 's business intelligence.

  • Integration with your systems and workflows

    Integrate artiManage with your databases. Then automate data processing, import, and export using an API.

  • Unified messaging

    An internal chat software for your company is the first step to official, yet easy communication. It's made better when the chat is easily accessible across any app in your company management system, as it is in artiManage.

  • Telephony

    Phone systems used for external calls can be easily integrated with artiManage through ArtiBridge, giving your teams click-to-call functionality, contextual information, and more.

  • SSO, provisioning, and access management

    Give access to multiple users and define their roles and permissions as you wish. You can also appoint a central administrator who's in charge of adding your employees and permissions. Single sign-on (SSO) can help your users use their credentials for all apps.
